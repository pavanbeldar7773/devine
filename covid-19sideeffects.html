Managing Long-Term Effects of COVID-19

**COVID-19** has left many individuals struggling with lingering health issues, commonly referred to as **"Long COVID"** or **Post-COVID Syndrome**. Even after recovering from the virus, some people experience ongoing symptoms affecting multiple organs, energy levels, and overall well-being. Our expert care focuses on identifying and treating these persistent effects, ensuring a smooth recovery.

"Recovery doesn’t end with a negative test—our specialized care helps you regain full health after COVID-19."

From respiratory difficulties to neurological issues, our tailored treatments help patients manage their symptoms and restore their quality of life. Whether you are experiencing fatigue, breathing problems, or mental health challenges post-COVID, we are here to guide your recovery journey.

Specialized Care for Post-COVID Health Issues

### **Common COVID-19 Side Effects**

  • Persistent fatigue and weakness
  • Breathing difficulties and lung damage
  • Loss of taste and smell
  • Memory loss and difficulty concentrating ("Brain Fog")
  • Joint and muscle pain
  • Heart complications such as palpitations
  • Digestive problems and nausea
  • Anxiety, depression, and mood swings

### **Our Treatment Approach**

  • **Respiratory Therapy:** Lung function tests, breathing exercises, and medication
  • **Energy Restoration:** Vitamin therapy, nutritional counseling, and lifestyle adjustments
  • **Cognitive Support:** Brain health treatments for memory loss and focus issues
  • **Heart & Vascular Care:** ECG monitoring and heart health management
  • **Mental Health Support:** Therapy for anxiety, depression, and stress relief
  • **Holistic Recovery:** Yoga, physiotherapy, and immunity-boosting programs

**Post-COVID recovery is unique for each individual.** Whether your symptoms are mild or severe, we provide **personalized treatment plans** to help you regain strength, restore lung function, and improve overall health.
